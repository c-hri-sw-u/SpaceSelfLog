Why 👓 Egocentric Vision?

We select egocentric vision—first-person visual capture from a body-worn device—as the primary physical-world input, supplemented where useful by audio and inertial data.

🚶 Mobility

Continuous capture across locations throughout the day, persisting as the user moves between spaces.

🍱 Information Density

A single frame reveals Environment (context), Activity (process), and Objects (entities) simultaneously.

👁️ Implicit Attention

The device's field-of-view serves as a proxy for human attention; in-frame implies active engagement.

🚀 Technical Maturity

Modern VLMs effectively project pixel-level data onto structured semantic logic spaces.
