Study Design

A two-phase framework supporting exploratory iteration on design variables and rigorous observation of agent behavior.

Phase 0: Calibration

Approx. 1 week · Active Iteration
⚙️

System Stabilization

Active tuning of over a dozen design variables spanning capture timings, pre-process thresholds, and prompt structures.

📝

Design Iteration Log

Documenting modifications, rationales, and before/after states to construct the primary dataset for emerging design implications.

Phase 1: Observation

2 Weeks · Frozen Parameters
👁️

Passive Observation

Using the agent organically for daily tasks to trace whether physical-world contexts surface without deliberate steering.

🎯

Structured Probes

Deploying 1–2 daily requests crafted around captured logs to evaluate agent utilization of physical traces when explicitly relevant.

⏰

Proactive Cron

Tracking the agent's autonomous judgment to autonomously trigger contact or deliberately decline acting on new physical-world activity updates.
