Design Rationale
  • Context accumulation. Every VLM call includes batch frames, metadata, and the previous call's result as context.
  • Batch, not stream. Grouping frames per-scene gives the VLM temporal context to reason about transitions.
  • Insight decoupling. A single batch rarely carries enough signal for a higher-level summary — that will be handled in the next layer.
