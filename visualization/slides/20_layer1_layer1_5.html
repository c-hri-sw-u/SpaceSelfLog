Design Rationale
Sending every frame to the cloud is prohibitively expensive.
Together these two layers compress ~2.6M frames/day down to ~300–600.
Only meaningful frames leave the device.
L1 Capture
Decides when to capture.
Audio & Motion triggers adjust Vision's interval. All sensors contribute tags.
L1.5 Preprocess
Decides what to keep.
Scene boundary detection, filtering, importance scoring, and smart frame selection.
