The Core Focus: Strategic Compression

Strategic Pervasiveness: Compressing is the most critical step in this project. Processing every raw frame is prohibitively expensive in power and cost—therefore, compression is pervasive throughout the entire pipeline.

Compression = Understanding: To compress is to understand. It requires selection, deconstruction, and reconstruction. Since this involves inherent information loss, the system must prioritize. Much like the human brain, which filters infinite sensory inputs to retain only the most meaningful, our pipeline must identify what matters.

Defining Key Events: The essence of compression is the intelligent identification of Key Events from the noise. These events are judged via two mechanisms:

  • Autonomous Reasoning: VLM/LLM-driven judgment guided by specialized system prompts.
  • Human Feedback: Real-time insights and corrections captured during active chat sessions.
