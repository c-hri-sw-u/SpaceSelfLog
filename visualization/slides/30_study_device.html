#1 Why an Off-the-Shelf Smartphone?
It has everything required natively: a high-quality camera, microphone, IMU, Wi-Fi, and reliable battery life. Developing a native iOS Swift application offers significant agility compared to building custom hardware from scratch or hacking closed smart-glasses ecosystems.
iPhone 13
A15 Bionic Chip
12MP Dual Cam
6-Axis IMU
Wi-Fi 6 & 3240mAh
Continuous capture App, developed in Swift
#2 Why Chest-Mounted?
Ultimately opted for the chest-mounted Option 2 over the head-mounted Option 1. While sacrificing some attentional cues from neck rotation, it provides Greater Stability and Enhanced Comfort. This allowed me to forget the device's presence completely and behave entirely naturally during the study.
